CNC Wood is a Computer Numerical Controlled (CNC) woodworking small business that specializes in designing and creating custom routed wood, brass and aluminum products for people in our community and local small businesses.

We have a solid customer base with the military, and can create customized plaques and items of personal recognition.

We can also provide fit and finish to our wood products, with wood engraving, painting and preserving.
